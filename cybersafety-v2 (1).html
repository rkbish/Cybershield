📚 What makes a strong password?

  • ✅ At least 12 characters long
  • ✅ Mix of UPPERCASE + lowercase
  • ✅ Include numbers (0–9)
  • ✅ Include symbols (@, #, !, $)
  • ❌ Never use your name or birthday
  • ❌ Never use common words like "password"
  • ❌ Never share your password with friends

    📚 Cyberbullying: What You Must Know

    What is it?
    Using technology to harass, threaten, or embarrass someone repeatedly.
    If it happens to you:
    Don't respond. Take screenshots. Block the bully. Tell a trusted adult immediately.
    If you see it:
    Don't laugh or share. Support the victim privately. Report to an adult or admin.
    In India:
    Cyberbullying can be reported under IT Act Section 66A. Helpline: 1930
