    📚 Cyberbullying: What You Must Know

    ⚠️ What is it?

    Repeated harassment, threats or humiliation using technology.

    ✅ If it happens to YOU:

    Don't respond. Screenshot. Block. Tell a trusted adult.

    👁️ If you SEE it:

    Don't laugh or share. Support the victim. Report it.

    🇮🇳 In India:

    Cyberbullying is covered under the IT Act. Helpline: 1930
